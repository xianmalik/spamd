Laser Hair Removal: What is it?

It’s that time of year again; summer. You are probably thinking about going to the beach. However, you just realized that you forgot to shave or wax your legs, arms or back! At SpaMD we offer laser hair removal for just those situations. If you’re unhappy with tweezing, waxing or any other method of hair removal, then laser hair removal may be just what you need. Laser hair removal is a very common procedure in the United States. Laser hair removal works by emitting a beam of highly concentrated light waves deep into the skin and hair follicles. Pigment in the skin then absorbs the light, and that in turn destroys the hair. We can’t wait to see you in for a free consultation for laser hair removal.
