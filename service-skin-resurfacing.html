Skin Resurfacing: What is it?

Laser skin resurfacing is a popular cosmetic procedure that can help reduce the effects the aging has on the skin. As we age the sun, chemicals, or our own oils can damage the skin on our face. Skin resurfacing works by diminishing the imperfections (or appearance of) on the skins surface. During the procedure, a laser is used to dissolve molecular bonds of damaged skin cells layer by layer until a smoother cleaner looking skin shows through. Laser skin resurfacing is known to be rejuvenating because of the numerous anti-aging benefits. You will be pleasantly surprised with the overall look and feel of your skin after the procedure.
