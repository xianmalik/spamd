Dermal Fillers: What are they?

Dermal fillers can be used in a variety of ways when it comes to facial aging. Dermal fillers work by injecting natural or synthetic compounds into the skin to “plump” that specific are. Once a dermal filler has been injected a wrinkle, depression or fold will begin to dissipate. These results can last anywhere from six months to two years, and in some cases, longer. Dermal Fillers should not be confused with Botox or any of our other neurotoxin injections. However, dermal fillers can be used in conjunction with neurotoxin injections to result in a dramatically younger looking face. Dermal fillers can add volume to the face and smooth out creases that run from the nose to the mouth. Dermal fillers can also plump lips, augment cheeks, and fill out depressions and fine lines. Dermal Fillers:
What are the benefits?

Dermal fillers have a plethora of advantages for your face. Dermal fillers have the ability to fill in laugh lines, plump thin lips and smooth out vertical lines on the edges of the mouth. Dermal fillers also augmented checks to have a rounder shape often resulting in a more youthful appearance. Dermal fillers even have the ability to make indented scars more even with the surrounding skin and fill out gauntness in the lower cheek or temple area.

Dermal Fillers all benefits:

Add volume to deep creases in the nose and mouth

Plumping thin lips

Smoothing vertical lines around the lips

Augmenting cheeks to enhance shape

Filling in eye depressions

Making acne scars less visible

Increasing volume of the lower check or temple area.
