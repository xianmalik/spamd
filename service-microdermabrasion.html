Microdermabrasion: What is it?

Microdermabrasion is one of those procedures that a lot of people have heard of, but don’t know much about. Microdermabrasion is a procedure that exfoliates and removes the very top layer of dry, dead skin cells. At SpaMD we use the latest in microdermabrasion technology. Our microdermabrasion machine will buff and polish the skin using a very fine stream of crystals. If you have fine lines, uneven pigmentation or clogged pores, then microdermabrasion might be the exact thing that your skin needs. Microdermabrasion tackles skin flaws at the outer most layer of the skin, so it is non-invasive, and the skin is not wounded or abraded in any way past the epidermis. This is ideal for anyone who doesn’t want to utilize harsher techniques on their skin. We can’t wait to see you in at SpaMD for a free consultation!

Microdermabrasion:
What are the benefits?

First and foremost, microdermabrasion is noninvasive, so there is no need to schedule out days of down time. Secondly, Microdermabrasion is mostly painless. The treatment itself lasts only an hour or so, and the results are nearly instantaneous. Microdermabrasion is also a useful procedure for all skin types and is perfect for people who have problems with sun damage, age spots, discoloration, and fine lines that can occur with aging. Microdermabrasion has also been known to aid in acne scarring and is known to be extremely safe and efficient.
