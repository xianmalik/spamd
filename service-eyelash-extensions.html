Eyelash Extensions: What are they?

Most women don’t have the time to apply fake eyelashes every day, so it’s no wonder that eyelash extensions have become so popular! There are three different types of eyelashes we use at SpaMD, synthetic, mink, and volume. When we apply our volume lashes we DO NOT use pre-made clusters; we tailor every eyelash to the individual. The sizes range from 6 mm to 17 mm. Once your desired length is selected, the lashes are applied using a semi-permanent glue that will not irritate the eye or damage the natural eyelash. At SpaMD we only use the best quality Lash Savvy eyelash extensions, they can be applied in around two hours, and maintenance routines every three to four weeks are recommended. We are professionals when it comes to applying Lash Savvy lashes, we use professional tools and take the ultimate care in each application. We cannot wait to see you in for a free consolation today. Let’s add some volume to those lashes!
