Kybella: What is it?

Kybella is the only FDA approved procedure to remove fat from under the chin. Kybella has the power to destroy fat cells under the skin using it’s patented, minimally invasive technology. Using Kybella, you can get rid of “double chin” which is the technical term for fat accumulated under the chin and jaw. Up until Kybella was invented double chin had been notoriously difficult to correct, and the only available procedure was liposculpture. Kybella targets the specific fat cells around the chin and will leave your jaw line looking tight and refreshed. Kybella is a relatively painless process; it involves injecting a compound of deoxycholic acid under the surface of the skin. This compound occurs naturally in the body and is very effective at absorbing and eliminating fat. We would love to see you in today for a free consultation to see if Kybella is right for you!
