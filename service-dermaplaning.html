Dermaplaning: What is it?

Dermaplaning is a skin treatment that removes dead skin cells and vellus hair. A clinician takes an incredibly sharp blade, holds the skin tight, and brushes using an upward motion. This skin procedure is noninvasive and painless. Most people say it feels like a baby’s hands running across your face; it’s quite enjoyable! This can be done on the face, forehead, chin, nose and neck. You will notice an immediate improvement in the texture and tone of the skin in the short term and decreased signs of aging, wrinkles, dark spots, and acne scarring long term. Dermaplaning will leave your skin with a fresh new glow. After dermaplaning you will also notice that topical treatments and products perform better because they penetrate the skin more easily. Dermaplaning:
What are the benefits?

Dermaplaning will leave you with glowing skin, smooth features, and wide awake eyes. Dermaplaning will give your skin an immediate glow that you will notice instantly. Anyone who wants a deep exfoliation of the skin or unwanted facial hair can benefit from dermaplaning. Dermaplaning is painless and noninvasive, so there is no down time associated with the procedure. Dermaplaning is also an excellent system for anyone with super sensitive skin or pregnant mothers who are unable to use certain exfoliating products.

All dermaplaning benefits include:

Helps exfoliate the skin

Offers better results than traditional microdermabrasion

Little to no downtime

Removes unwanted hair

Can be performed with a chemical peel
